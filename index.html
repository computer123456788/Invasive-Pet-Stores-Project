Invasive Pet Stores Project

What is the Invasive Pet Stores Project?

The Invasive Pet Stores Project is a project by students to help people know that the stores they buy from are dedicated to protecting the enviorment

What are invasive species?

Invasive species are species that are not native to the enviorment that they are in and take over pushing out native species

Why are invasive species bad?

Invasive species are bad because they push out native species and disrupt ecosystems

How are invasive species introduced into ecosystems?

Invasive species enter new ecosystems through many different ways a big one being the pet trade

What can I do?

If you are a customer then you can check if they sell invasive species and ask them to get verified with the Invasive Pet Stores Project or you can get certified and sign off on pet stores that are not selling invasive species

If you are a pet store contact us and we will inspect and sign off on your store giving you a certificate stating that your petstore is invasive species free
